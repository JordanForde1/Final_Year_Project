Data visualisation of Referenda held in Ireland and Polling System for upcoming Referenda

This web application is a polling system used to gather information around the country of Ireland to allow campaign parties that are taking part in these referenda to see concentrations of votes along with a variety of other information about the public. This information is displayed as a series of graphs, progress bars and heatmaps. The data that has been gathered in this poll can be compared to the data of previous referedna held in Ireland
